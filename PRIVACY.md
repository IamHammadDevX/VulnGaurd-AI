# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 11, 2026

## 1. Introduction

VulnGaurd-AI ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our vulnerability scanning and assessment service (the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, password, and team/organization details
- **Scan Data:** Code repositories, dependencies, configuration files, and project metadata submitted for vulnerability analysis
- **Authentication Data:** Login credentials, API keys, and authentication tokens
- **Communication Data:** Messages, support requests, and feedback you provide to us

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage Data:** Features accessed, scan frequency, search queries, interaction patterns, and time spent
- **Device Information:** Browser type, operating system, device type, and unique device identifiers
- **Network Data:** IP address, referral source, and pages visited
- **Cookies and Tracking Technologies:** Web beacons, pixels, and local storage for session management and analytics

### 2.3 Third-Party Data

- **AI Provider Integration:** When using AI-powered analysis features, certain scan data may be processed by third-party AI providers (e.g., Anthropic)
- **Authentication Services:** SSO and OAuth provider data (e.g., Replit Auth) for authentication purposes

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

- **Service Delivery:** Performing vulnerability scans, generating reports, and providing security assessments
- **Improvement:** Analyzing usage patterns to enhance features and user experience
- **Communication:** Sending service updates, security alerts, and support responses
- **Legal Compliance:** Meeting regulatory requirements and enforcing terms of service
- **Security:** Detecting fraud, preventing abuse, and protecting system integrity
- **AI Model Training:** *Anonymized and aggregated data only* may be used to improve AI detection models (see section 3.1)

### 3.1 AI and Machine Learning

- Scan data is processed through AI services to identify vulnerabilities
- We do not use your proprietary code to train models without explicit consent
- AI processing may involve third-party providers as detailed in section 5

## 4. Data Retention

- **Active Accounts:** Data retained while your account is active and for the duration of any contractual commitment
- **Inactive Accounts:** After account closure, personal data is retained for 90 days, then deleted unless legal obligations require longer retention
- **Scan Results:** Historical scan data retained for 12 months by default; configurable per team settings
- **Backup Data:** Encrypted backups may be retained for up to 30 days after deletion
- **Legal Holds:** Data may be retained longer if required by law or legal proceedings

We share data with:

### 5.4 No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

## 6. Data Security

### 6.1 Security Measures

We implement:
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- TLS 1.3+ encryption for data in transit
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) support
- ISO 27001 compliance measures
- Row-level security (RLS) in databases

### 6.2 Limitations

No security system is impenetrable. While we implement comprehensive safeguards, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining confidentiality of your credentials.
